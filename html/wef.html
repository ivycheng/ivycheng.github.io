Whole Earth Festival

Print & Marketing

About

The Whole Earth Festival is an annual festival held in Davis, California, that celebrates ecological sustainability through education, music, food, and art. As the program coordinator of the festival, I was responsible for creating marketing materials, which include newspaper advertisements, programs, and signs. The main challenge that I faced was our limited budget for print materials. I had to carefully strategize my usage of space and color to convey all the information in the most space and cost-efficient way possible.

Program Coordinator, University of California, Davis.
