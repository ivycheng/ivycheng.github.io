Design Career Fair

Branding and Event Marketing

About

My colleagues and I organized a design-oriented career fair – the first in UC Davis history – for Spring 2015. With the support of the UC Davis design department, we sponsored companies specializing in Visual Communication, Interior, Fashion, and Product Design. As a member of the Marketing team, I was responsible for designing and creating effective branding and marketing materials. Classroom announcements and social media marketing were also our priorities to maximize publicity.
